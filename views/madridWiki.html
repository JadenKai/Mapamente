Madrid

History

Madrid was founded in the late 19th century. It was originally named Mayrit as an Islamic fortress on the Manzanares River.

Current Facts

Madrid is the capital of Spain and the largest city in Spain. The official symbol is “The Bear and the Strawberry Tree” (El Oso y El Madroño).
